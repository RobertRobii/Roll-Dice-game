Game's rules:

1. First to 50 wins.
2. When you press the "roll dice" button, the game starts.
3. When you press the "hold" button, the current score will be added to the total score and the players will switch.
4. When the dice equals 1, your current score will go down to 0 and the players will switch.

Good luck!
